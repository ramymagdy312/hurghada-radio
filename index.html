Who We Are?

What we do?

We strive to deliver a unique and distinctive radio experience to every listener. We offer a diverse range of entertaining programs and carefully curated music to delight all tastes. Whether you're looking for music to unwind or talk shows to engage with, we're here to cater to your radio needs in an enjoyable and uplifting manner. Explore with us the different facets of life in Hurghada, and join us on a journey filled with music and entertainment.

Why choose us?

We understand that you have many options when it comes to choosing a radio station. So why should you choose us? Simply put, because we're dedicated to providing you with an unmatched listening experience. From our carefully curated music playlists to our engaging talk shows and timely news updates, we go above and beyond to ensure that you're entertained, informed, and connected to the pulse of Hurghada. With our commitment to quality programming and our passion for serving our community, choosing us means choosing excellence in radio broadcasting.

Where we are?

Here! we want to make it easy for you to find us. Located in the heart of Hurghada, Egypt, we're just a click away. Whether you're tuning in from the comfort of your home, streaming on your smartphone while on the go, or anywhere else, our online radio station is accessible anytime, anywhere. Stay connected with us and enjoy the vibrant rhythms of Hurghada, no matter where you are in the world.
